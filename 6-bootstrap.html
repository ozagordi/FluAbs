Bootstrapping some of the relations

AIC score is defined as \[ AIC = -2\, \log \textrm{likelihood} + 2\, k \] where \( k \) is the number of parameters in the model. This means the lower, the better. In the following, the full model has \( k=3 \), no interaction has \( k=2 \) and both age only and shot only have \( k=1 \).
